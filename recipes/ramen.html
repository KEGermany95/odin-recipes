Ramen

Ramen is a noodle-based dished widely regarded as a Japanese soul food. It is consumed in cold or warm weather.

The word ramen is a Japanese borrowing of the Mandarin Chinese lāmiàn. The dish is an adaptation of of Chinese wheat noodles.

Ingredients

  • dry noodles
  • broth
  • seaweed
  • egg

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil pasta to preferred firmenss
  2. Slowly lower noodles into broth
  3. Lay seaweed on side of bowl adjecent to ramen
  4. Slice egg and place in bowl
  5. Serve
